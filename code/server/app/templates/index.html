NewsPhi

An Aid For Contextual News Consumption

Our Mission

Our mission is to raise awareness for readers of controversial news topics and reputation of writers.

The critical analysis service we provide will include a controversy score of the subject/topic of the article.

Our aim is to encourage readers to select news they want to read instead of just giving them what to read.

We provide a platform to warn users of which news topics to be aware.

Our Team
